►Who are we?

Kolpu Valley Krishi Farm is an agricultural startup initiated by Manoj Mudvari in 2018. Being a multipurpose organization, our vision is production, processing and marketing of agriculture.

We are located on Kumari, Belkotgadhi Muncipility-03 of Nuwakot District. We have a wide range of activities from Fruits/Vegetable production to livestock rearing. We try to promote agriculture as a secure profession for youths and make them self sustained in their homeland. Our target is to contribute to the economy of the nation through a revolution in agriculture.

Our target is to provide products to our customers at a very affordable rate by minimizing the role of middle-man. We try to minimize the gap between agricultural producers and end-user. This would lead to a substantial decrease in price for customers and ensure welfare for farmers.
